## 📖 Overview

The **International Student Academic Support Guide** is a web-based resource designed to help international students navigate academic life and succeed in a new educational environment.

It brings together essential academic resources, practical tips, and support systems into one clean, accessible platform to make the transition smoother and less overwhelming.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📚 Academic resources and study strategies  
- 🧠 Learning support (tutoring, writing help, etc.)  
- 🌐 Focus on international student challenges  
- 📝 Tips on communication, assignments, and expectations  
- 🎯 Clean, minimal, user-friendly design  

---

- HTML5  
- CSS3

## 🚀 Purpose

- Support international students academically
