🌿 Counseling & Wellness Services

Adjusting to a new academic system, culture, and environment can feel overwhelming. Stress, homesickness, anxiety, and burnout are common — even when classes are going well. This is a completely normal part of the international student experience.

Counseling & Wellness Services provides confidential support for emotional well-being, academic stress, and life transitions. Seeking support is a healthy and proactive step.

🗣 Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions with a licensed counselor to talk through anything you're experiencing.

👥 Group Support

Workshops and group sessions where you can connect with others facing similar challenges.

🧘 Stress Management

Practical coping strategies and wellness resources for managing academic and personal stress.

🚨 Walk-in & Crisis Support

Immediate support available when you need it most — no appointment necessary for walk-ins.

Location: 053 Student Union
